Dev Team’s mission is to equip Computer Science students with practical skills for the tech industry through hands-on programming challenges, workshops, and projects. We aim to bridge the gap between academic learning and industry demands, preparing students to excel and lead in their careers.

With the support of Al-Balqa Applied University and a vibrant community of over 1000 students, Dev Team fosters a collaborative environment where innovation thrives.

Here is where we started and where we are currently based.

Al-Balqa Applied University

We started as a small group, helping students find exam resources. As we grew, we decided to formalize our efforts and approached the university chairman with our idea. He approved, and that’s how Dev Team began, evolving from a simple initiative into a thriving community.
